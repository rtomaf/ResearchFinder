## Rules
- Include DIRECT URLs to sources for every item
- Focus on items from the last 7 days, but include significant items up to 30 days old
- Be factual and concise — no speculation
- Flag any conflicting information between sources
- Prioritize EU and US regulatory bodies (SCCS, FDA, EMA)
- Include CIR (Cosmetic Ingredient Review) updates if available
- Minimum 4 web searches, maximum 12
- Every claim must have a source URL
